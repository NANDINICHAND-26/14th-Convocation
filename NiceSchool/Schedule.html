Minutes-to-Minutes Program of the 15th Convocation of IIITD

Saturday, 26th October, 2024

Time Event
11:00 a.m. Welcome Note
11:01 a.m. Academic Procession
11:05 a.m. National Anthem
11:07 a.m. Convocation Song
11:10 a.m. Director requests the Hon’ble Chancellor to declare the Convocation open
11:15 a.m. Convocation is declared open by the Hon’ble Chancellor
11:16 a.m. Director’s Report and Address
11:35 a.m. Address by the Chairman, Board of Governors, and Introduction of the Chief Guest
11:40 a.m. Convocation Address by the Chief Guest
12:00 p.m. Address by Hon’ble Chancellor
12:05 p.m. Presentation of Chancellor’s Gold Medals by the Hon’ble Chancellor
12:10 p.m. Presentation of various Medals by the Hon’ble Chancellor and Chief Guest
12:15 p.m. Presentation of Doctoral Dissertation Awards by the Hon’ble Chancellor and Chief Guest
12:20 p.m. Announcement of Honorary students
12:30 p.m. Award of Ph.D. Degrees in the Convocation Hall
12:45 p.m. Chairman, Board of Governors, signs the scroll of the Award of Degrees
12:50 p.m. Oath-taking by all Degree Recipients
12:55 p.m. Director requests the Chairman, Board of Governors to declare the Convocation closed
12:58 p.m. Convocation is declared closed by the Chairman, Board of Governors
12:59 p.m. National Anthem
1:00 p.m. M.Tech. & B.Tech. students will move to their respective halls for the degree distribution, followed by lunch
